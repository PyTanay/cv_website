Projects

Here are some of the largest prjects I have been involved with during my experience.

Formic Acid Plant Design

At GNFC Ltd. we already have Formic Acid plant and based on that we implemented a separate production stream running in parallel with the current stream to tackle bottlenecks in the current plant and increase production using waste-gases from the existing plant. All the basic engineering and most process aspects of detailed engineering were carried out by us.

We carried out material, mass and energy balance of the new plant and also the existing plant. Existing production stream was simulated with the help of Aspen Plus and HTRI (For HE design) and based on the limitation observed, new stream was designed to take care of increased production as well as current limitations. All the utilities like cooling water, Steam and Chilled brine networks were designed. Currently the project is under implementation stage.

Being part of core process engineering team I got exposure to almost all process engineering aspects like process design, process modeling and simulation, process design of all unit operations and equipments (like distillation columns, heat exchangers, control valves, safety valves etc.), datasheet preparation, P&ID preparation and utility network design. Also I participated in HAZOP study.

Aniline Plant Distillation Revamp

Aniline plant was facing the issues of the disturbed product quality at higher loads and that was causing the bottleneck. The issue was refered to the technical services team and we simulated the distillation section and we identified two limiting constraints where identified. Two different distillation columns were having high flooding in separate regions and based on the steady state column simulatons innovative solutions were implemented in both columns.

These modification removed the bottleneck and production capacity was increased by 25% resultion in significant increase in profit without need of much investment for revamping the whole section. The modification suggested also earned our depatment an award for the innovative idea.

As part of the core team I simulated the distillation section using Aspen Plus and all exchangers using HTRI to check the adequacy and pressure drop across them. All other peripheral equipments like pumps, control valves and safety valves were also checked.

Toluene Di-isocynate Heat Integration Project

In TDI plant stream with extra energy was identified and it was suggested to utilize this energy in heating up another stream going as distillation column feed, resultiong in significant reduction in the reboiler heat duty.

As part of this project I simulated both stream loop piping including all equipments involved to estimate accurate pressure profile as heating up of the other stream was causing the two phase flow in that stream and for the given service two phase flow was not recommended. I designed new heat exchanger for this service. Being the speciality chemical properties of these chemicals were not available in the simulators. so rigorous property parameters' regressions were done to match the properites at all temperature and pressure and using this regressed properties new exchanger was designed.
